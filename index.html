Universal Food Bag: Symbol & Color Guide

📝 Symbol & Color Meanings

This guide explains the universal symbols and colors used on the food identification bag to help people from any language or literacy level understand food information.

🍚

Green Circle with Rice Bowl

Indicates the main food item - rice. Green symbolizes safety and natural food.

🥚

Yellow Circle with Egg

Indicates the food contains protein. Yellow symbolizes energy and nutrition.

🥦

Red Circle with Broccoli

Indicates the food contains vitamins. Red symbolizes health and essential nutrients.

♿

Blue Circle with Wheelchair

Indicates the food is suitable for people with disabilities. Blue symbolizes accessibility.

🌍 Universal Design Principles

The food bag uses a combination of colors and symbols that are universally understood across cultures:

  • Colors: Selected for their universal associations (green for safety, yellow for energy, red for health, blue for accessibility)
  • Symbols: Simple, recognizable icons that transcend language barriers
  • Layout: Clear, consistent placement of information for easy understanding
  • Accessibility: Designed to be understood by people of all ages, abilities, and literacy levels

🌱 Purpose & Impact

This universal food identification system helps:

  • Refugees and displaced persons understand food information without language barriers
  • Illiterate individuals make informed decisions about food usage
  • Emergency responders distribute food more effectively during disasters
  • Reduce food waste by ensuring proper usage
  • Support global efforts to achieve Zero Hunger
